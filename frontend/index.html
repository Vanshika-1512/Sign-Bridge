How it works

Three steps to understanding

📷

Capture

Your webcam streams live video. We process each frame locally — your data never leaves your device.

🧠

Detect

MediaPipe extracts 21 hand landmarks per frame. Our ML model classifies the gesture in milliseconds.

📝

Translate

Predicted letters build into words. Optionally, the Web Speech API reads them aloud for you.
